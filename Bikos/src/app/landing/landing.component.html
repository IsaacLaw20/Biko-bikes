Generation / Storage / Loads (GAIA-GSL)

Introducing the GAIA-GSL - a state-of-the-art single-page application specifically designed for renewable equipment providers to optimise, benchmark, and promote their cutting-edge products. GAIA-GSL offers multi-aspect modelling and evaluation of consumer utility, carbon emissions, and lifetime costs based on user-specific home models, power usage demand, solar illuminance, external temperature, battery sizes, and energy prices.

GAIA-GSL streamlines the development process for renewable equipment providers by allowing them to input unique parameters and obtain in-depth insights into the efficiency, cost-effectiveness, and environmental impact of their products. With its advanced algorithms and real-time data integration, GAIA-GSL accurately projects the performance of various energy systems, enabling providers to tailor their solutions for maximum customer satisfaction.

Key Features:

  1. User-specific home modelling: Input customer home specifications to generate precise energy simulations, demonstrating the effectiveness of your products in real-world scenarios.
  2. Power usage demand analysis: Showcase your product's ability to meet a wide range of energy consumption patterns through intelligent solar and storage solutions.
  3. Solar illuminance assessment: Highlight the potential solar energy generation capabilities of your offerings, taking into account shading, orientation, and other factors.
  4. External temperature consideration: Demonstrate the resilience and adaptability of your products by evaluating their performance under varying temperatures.
  5. Customisable battery sizes: Emphasise the flexibility of your energy storage solutions by exploring a range of battery options suitable for different customer needs.
  6. Real-time energy price tracking: Promote cost savings and long-term value by incorporating the latest energy prices into your product simulations.
